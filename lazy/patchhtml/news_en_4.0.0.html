4.0.3 Foundry TDE News (8/30/2022)

Foundry v10

Foundry v10 has arrived. We made all necessary underlying changes to the code (thousands of changes; especially thanks to VTTom for lots of testing) and incorporated some of the new features.
The highlights of v10 include improved journals, vision modes, tours and many other things. This will also conclude our support for v9. Any future update and module will only be available from v10+. Due to the hight amount of required changes, we unfortunately expect some more bugs in early TDE 4.x.x versions.

Endless fixes

We made endless fixes and improvements to all modules and contents available. Among plenty of tickets raised on github, we also improved all our automated tests more and more to find inconsistencies and update the data to enable and add more possible future ideas.
