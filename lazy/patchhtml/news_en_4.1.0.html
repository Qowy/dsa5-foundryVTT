Upcoming Contents

Rabenkrieg 3 - Der Sturz des Adlers (German)

At the beginning of the third adventure of the Raven War campaign for Foundry VTT, the heroes have proven themselves reliable and prudent enough to receive their next task from Oderin du Metuant himself. Soon they find themselves in moral quandaries and may have to resort to rather unorthodox means to fulfill the Black General's will after all.

Raven War 3 - The Fall of the Eagle can also be played separately from the campaign background and is suitable for a group of 3 - 6 heroes.

Available soon at the F-Shop.

Verhandlungskunst und Friedenschwur

The module includes three diplomatic adventures for Foundry VTT. The heroes are faced with the task of using negotiation skills to prevent conflicts and solve problems amicably.

The collection of adventures was created in connection with a charity action in favor of Ukraine and was also implemented for Foundry VTT.

Available soon at the F-Shop.

Aventurisches Elementarium

The Aventurian adepts of elemental magic summon and control wondrous beings. The Aventurian Elementarium for Foundry VTT introduces more than 40 entities, including elemental spirits, djinns, and masters, as well as un-elementals and corrupted ones, their demonically corrupted variants.

The module also contains detailed rules for summoning and controlling elemental entities for player characters and master characters. It provides insight into the history of elementalism and offers six varied adventure settings.

Available soon at the F-Shop.
